Terms & Policy

1. Introduction
This General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) privacy policy outlines the procedures and conditions under which (hereinafter referred to as "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and manages personal data in compliance with GDPR guidelines. Our commitment to protecting your data is paramount, and we endeavor to handle all personal data with utmost care, transparency, and in accordance with legal standards.

This policy explains the specific terms regarding data usage, storage, and the necessary user approvals required for data retention, as well as the procedures in place for users who no longer wish their data to be processed. By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

2. Data Collection and Use
We collect and process personal data in accordance with GDPR, which includes the following types of information:

  • Personal identification data such as your name, email address, contact number, etc.
  • Usage data related to how you interact with our services and website.
  • Any other relevant information that may be required to deliver our services effectively.

The primary purpose for collecting your data is to provide you with optimal service experiences, improved functionality, and personalized communication. We use your data to:

  • Ensure the smooth operation of our services.
  • Enhance user experience and customize service offerings.
  • Communicate with you, send updates, promotions, and service-related information.
  • Analyze user behavior to further improve and develop our services.

    3.Data Retention Periods and User Consent Initial Data Retention Period
    Upon first collecting your personal data, we will retain this data for a period of 30 days. After this 30-day period, we will request your explicit consent to continue using your personal data. We believe in respecting user autonomy, and no data will be retained beyond this point unless we receive your consent.

    3.2 Renewed Consent After 11 Months
    If you provide consent for data retention after the initial 30-day period, your data will be stored for a maximum of 11 months from the date of consent.

    Before this period expires, you will receive a notification requesting renewed consent for the continued use of your personal data.

    This process will be repeated annually, requiring users to re-approve data usage every 11 months.

    If you choose not to renew your consent after 11 months, we will automatically stop processing your data, and it will be scheduled for deletion as outlined in section 5 of this policy.

    4. User Rights Under GDPR
    GDPR grants several rights to users regarding their personal data.

    As a user of our services, you have the following rights:
    • Right to Access: You may request access to the personal data we hold about you and obtain a copy of that information.
    • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
    • Right to Erasure (Right to Be Forgotten): You can request the deletion of your personal data, as detailed in section 5.
    • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request the restriction of data processing under certain circumstances.
    • Right to Data Portability: You can request your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
      • Right to Object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.

    To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at [admin@yourcompany.com].

    5. Data Deletion and Withdrawal
    Data Deletion and Withdrawal of Consent At any point, you can request that we cease processing and delete your personal data. To facilitate this, we have established the following process:

    Contacting the Admin for Data Deletion: If you decide that you no longer wish for us to retain or use your data, you can contact our admin via email at [admin@yourcompany.com].
    Please include the subject line "Data Deletion Request" along with your identifying information to expedite the process.

    Once we receive your request, we will:
    • Confirm receipt of your request within 7 working days.
    • Automatically delete all personal data associated with your account within 30 days of receiving the request.
    • Provide confirmation once the deletion has been successfully completed.

    After your data has been deleted, we will no longer process or store any personal information related to you.
    Please note that once the deletion is complete, you will no longer have access to your account or any associated services.

    If there are legal reasons that prevent us from deleting your data immediately (e.g., for tax or accounting purposes), we will inform you accordingly and explain the period for which the data will be retained.

    6. Data Security We are committed to ensuring that your personal data is kept secure.
    We implement a variety of security measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. These measures include:
    • Encryption:
      All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest to prevent unauthorized access.
    • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to personal data, and access is granted on a need-to-know basis.
    • Regular Audits:
      We regularly review and update our security practices to ensure compliance with industry standards and to prevent any potential vulnerabilities.

    In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your personal data, we will notify you and the relevant data protection authorities within 72 hours, as required under GDPR.

    7. Third-Party Data Sharing
    We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties, except under the following circumstances:
    • Service Providers:
      We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in delivering our services.
      These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and GDPR compliance.
    • Requirements: required by law, we may disclose personal data to comply with legal obligations or to protect our rights, safety, or the safety of others.

    Whenever we engage third-party service providers, we ensure that they adhere to the same level of data protection and privacy standards required under GDPR.

    8. Data Processing Outside the EU
    In certain cases, your data may be processed or stored outside the European Economic Area (EEA). In such instances, we ensure that appropriate safeguards, such as the use of Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or Binding Corporate Rules (BCRs), are in place to protect your personal data in line with GDPR requirements.
